Hello!

I made a map for Drehmal 2.1. Now this is the new & upgraded version of this website! On the 👈left you can find a menu multiple versions of the map. I hope you enjoy this, as much as I do 😁
All these maps are highly spoiler-y and should be treated as such. Please use use spoiler tags on Discord 😊
You have several options of maps in the 👈menu:
  • Game Maps - The least detailed map, only shows maps from items that exist in the game.
  • Isometric Map - The most detailed map. Spawns, locations, items and a lot of basically everything is (optionally) listed here.
  • 2D Map - Shows the whole world, but has no hints.
  • 3D Map - Makes the whole world explorable, but has no hints (yet?).
